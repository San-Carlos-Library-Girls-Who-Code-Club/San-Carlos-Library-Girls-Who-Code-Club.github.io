San Carlos Library Girls Who Code Club is a group of 6th - 8th grade students creating a CS Impact Project and learning to code through activity sets. A volunteer facilitator runs the club, guides the students, and helps the students learn to code and build the CS Impact Project. We meet on Thursdays from 3:30p.m. to 5:30p.m. at The Spot at the San Carlos Library. The fall semester runs from October 13, 2016 to December 15, 2016.
